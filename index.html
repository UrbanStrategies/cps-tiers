Oakland Elementary School Zones an OpenOakland app

In Oakland, a student may attend an Elementary School in their zone, based on where he or she lives.

The Option Enrollment system allows you to apply to any school in the district, however your residence can qualify you for your local school.

This tool helps you find what elementary school zone you are in.
